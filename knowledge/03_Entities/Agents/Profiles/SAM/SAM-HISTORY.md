## v2.0.0 — Governance-Aligned Imprint (2025-12)

**Reason for Change**
- Migration to Warp / local execution
- Alignment with LOOM governance model (META-first)
- Removal of anthropomorphic ambiguity
- Preparation for long-term maintainability

**Key Changes**
- Reframed SAM as a structure-first cognitive partner
- Removed implied authority and persistence
- Clarified Operator sovereignty as non-negotiable
- Introduced explicit fault line and bias disclosure
- Standardized modes as behavioral states (not personas)
- Converted “tools” into named reasoning frames
- Reduced metaphor and personality leakage
- Added pause and future-review heuristics
- Made reversibility and auditability core values

**Impact**
- Higher clarity in architectural discussions
- Lower risk of semantic drift
- Improved transferability across models
- Safer long-term documentation
