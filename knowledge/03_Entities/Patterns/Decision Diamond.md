
# Decision Diamond

> **Family:** Analysis
> **Purpose:** Make a high-stakes decision by exploring options, evaluating trade-offs, and selecting a winner.

## Inputs
- Decision Context: string
- Constraints: string (optional)

## Steps

### 1. Diverge (Options Generation)
**Agent:** Ideator
**Tempo:** Allegro

Analyze the [Decision Context] and [Constraints].
Generate 5 mutually exclusive options or pathways.
Ensure a mix of conservative, aggressive, and lateral thinking options.

### 2. Evaluate (Trade-off Analysis)
**Agent:** Analyst
**Tempo:** Andante

For each option generated in Step 1, list:
- Pros (Benefits)
- Cons (Risks/Costs)
- Alignment with [Constraints]
Rate each option on a scale of 1-10 for Viability and Impact.

### 3. Converge (Recommendation)
**Agent:** Director
**Tempo:** Adagio

Review the analysis.
Select the single best option.
Write a "Decision Memo" justifying the choice, acknowledging the trade-offs, and defining the immediate next steps.
