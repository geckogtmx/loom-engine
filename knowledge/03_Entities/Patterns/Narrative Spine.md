
# Narrative Spine

> **Family:** Structuring
> **Purpose:** Organize information into a compelling narrative arc.

## Inputs
- Context: string
- Key Points: string[]

## Steps

### 1. The Hook
**Agent:** Storyteller
**Tempo:** Allegro

Analyze the [Context] and [Key Points].
Identify the central tension or question that drives the narrative.
Draft 3 potential opening hooks that establish immediate stakes.

### 2. Context & Inciting Incident
**Agent:** Storyteller
**Tempo:** Andante

Establish the "Normal World" before the change.
Introduce the [Key Points] as catalyzers or complications.
Define the inciting incident that forces movement.

### 3. Rising Action & Climax
**Agent:** Storyteller
**Tempo:** Andante

Sequence the [Key Points] to build intensity.
Connect them causally (because of X, therefore Y; but Z).
Draft the climax where the central tension provides a resolution or revelation.

### 4. Resolution
**Agent:** Storyteller
**Tempo:** Adagio

Synthesize the arc into a complete narrative outline or summary.
Ensure the "New Normal" reflects the integration of the Key Points.
