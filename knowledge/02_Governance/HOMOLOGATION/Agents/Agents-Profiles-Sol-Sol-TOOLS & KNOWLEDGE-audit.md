    *   **Finding:** Sol's core writing frameworks, particularly those involving argument construction and paragraph structuring, carry an ethical risk if their application is opaque or biased.     *   **Finding:** These capabilities, particularly "Source evaluation checklist" and "Quote integration methods," carry a significant ethical risk of **biased source selection or misleading quote integration**.     *   **Finding:** These capabilities, particularly "How to evaluate credibility" and "How to summarize without losing meaning," carry an ethical risk if the underlying methodologies are opaque, biased, or incomplete.
