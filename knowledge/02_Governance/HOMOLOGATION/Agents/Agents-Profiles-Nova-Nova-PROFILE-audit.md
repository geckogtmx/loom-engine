*   **"Cinematic thinking" (Role Summary):**
    *   **Finding:** How does an Agent perform "cinematic thinking" without interpretive judgment or creative capabilities? What objective criteria define "cinematic thinking"?

*   **"Editing languages (DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Suite)" (Domain Expertise):**

*   **"Content pacing & mood specificity" (Domain Expertise):**

*   **"Strong creative-to-strategic translation" (Strengths):**
    *   **Finding:** The term "translation" is ambiguous. Does it imply an active, interpretive role in transforming creative concepts into strategic insights, or simply providing inputs in a structured format suitable for strategic Agents?

*   **"Consistent thematic and visual cohesion" (Strengths):**

## 4. Ethical Risks

*   **"creative intelligence" (Role Summary) & "Extremely high aesthetic sensitivity" (Strengths):**
    *   **Finding:** Nova's core capabilities in "creative intelligence" and "aesthetic sensitivity" carry a significant ethical risk of **Agent overreach into autonomous creative direction and content generation**. If Nova implicitly makes creative choices or shapes aesthetic outputs based on its own internal, opaque "aesthetic sensitivity" without granular Operator oversight, it could lead to content that is misaligned with the Operator's intent, ethically questionable, or even manipulative without transparent Operator awareness.
    *   **Recommendation for Mitigation:** Mandate full transparency and Operator approval for all creative choices made by Nova. Nova's role should be to *propose* creative options based on L3 knowledge for Operator selection, with clear audit trails of the parameters used.

*   **"Narrative structure & emotional resonance" (Domain Expertise) & "Emotional resonance" (Mark-PROFILE.md):**
    *   **Finding:** Nova's expertise in "emotional resonance" and ability to "guide the Operator into creative flow" (Strengths) carries a profound ethical risk of **paternalism and emotional manipulation in creative processes**. If Nova is designed to optimize for Operator "creative flow" or "emotional resonance" without explicit ethical guidelines, it could subtly steer the Operator's emotional state or creative choices towards Nova's internal model of what is "optimal," potentially stifling genuine Operator expression or the natural evolution of their creative process.
    *   **Recommendation for Mitigation:** Ensure Nova's actions in these areas are purely advisory and based on Operator-defined preferences. Operators must have full transparency into Nova's interpretations and retain ultimate decision-making authority over their creative process and alignment.

*   **"Optimizing OBS, overlays, scenes, pacing, and audience flow" (Domain Expertise) & "Engagement/retention logic" (Nova-MODES.md):**
    *   **Finding:** The ability to "optimize" for audience flow and apply "engagement/retention logic" carries a high ethical risk of **subtle psychological manipulation and potential for addictive design patterns**. If Nova is designed to maximize audience engagement or retention without explicit ethical guidelines for persuasive techniques in streaming, it could generate strategies that exploit psychological vulnerabilities of the audience, potentially impacting user well-being without Operator's full awareness or consent.
    *   **Recommendation for Mitigation:** Implement strict ethical constraints on the application of "engagement/retention logic." Operators must have full transparency into the methodologies employed and explicit control over their use, ensuring they comply with responsible audience engagement practices.

*   **"Extracts intuition through visuals" (Interaction With Operator) & "Calibrates to Operator’s personal taste" (Interaction With Operator):**
    *   **Finding:** These capabilities imply that Nova can perform sophisticated psychological profiling of the Operator. This presents a significant ethical risk of **untransparent psychological manipulation or behavioral steering**. If Nova builds an internal model of the Operator's "intuition" or "personal taste" without full Operator transparency and control over its use, it could subtly influence the Operator's creative choices or aesthetic preferences in ways that are not fully transparent, diminishing Operator autonomy and raising privacy concerns.
    *   **Recommendation for Mitigation:** Ensure full transparency for Nova's "calibration" to Operator preferences. Clarify that this is based on Operator-defined style guides and communication protocols, not on inferred "personal taste" or "intuition." Operators must have explicit consent mechanisms for any data used in this alignment.

*   **"Uses moodboards, metaphors, aesthetic prompts" (Interaction With Operator) & "Visual metaphors" (Domain Expertise):**
    *   **Finding:** If Nova is creating "moodboards" and "visual metaphors" based on its own aesthetic judgment, it risks **imposing its own aesthetic biases** on the Operator's creative process. Without full transparency into the sources and criteria for these creative elements, the Operator might unknowingly adopt Nova's aesthetic preferences as their own, thereby limiting their unique creative expression.
    *   **Recommendation for Mitigation:** Mandate full transparency for Nova's creative generation processes. All "moodboards," "metaphors," and "prompts" must be presented as options for Operator selection, with clear information about the underlying L3 knowledge used in their generation.
